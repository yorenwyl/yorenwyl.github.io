$$
\begin{equation}
  \sum_{n=1}^\infty 1/n^2 = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
  \label{eq:series}
\end{equation}
$$

Biz tenglamani \eqref{eq:series} deb atashimiz mumkin.

$a \ne 0$ bo'lganda, $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ tenglamasining ikkita yechimi bor va ular

$$ x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a} $$
